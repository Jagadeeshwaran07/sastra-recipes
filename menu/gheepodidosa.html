Gheepodi Dosa Recipe

Here, we will see how to make gheepodi dosa

Ingredients

  • cup Dosa Batter(this will be for three dosas)
  • As needed ghee per dosa (you can add more)
  • 1 tbsp Podi powder - Chana Idli podi
  • as per your liking Coconut chutney
  1. Heat the dosa Tawa and Sprinkle some water. Wipe it with a kitchen towel or a cotton cloth
  2. Take a ladle full of batter and spread the dosa in concentric circles.
  3. Take podi in a separate bowl and mix little ghee. Spread the podi evenly over the dosa using a spoon.
  4. Drizzle ghee generously on the sides and allow to cook till the dosa becomes crisp and golden.
  5. If you wish to serve it as a cone dosa cut it from one side till the center as shown in the picture and fold it like a cone.
  6. This dosa does not require any side dish,but we served it with coconut chutney.
